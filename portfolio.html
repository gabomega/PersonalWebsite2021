School Lab

This is a physics lab on torque that I completed at home during the 2020-2021 school year. I had to calculate the torque and create a balance beam using various objects. Here I used a wallet with coins inside, hand sanitizer and chapstick connected using a hair tie, and an eyelash curler as my objects. I positioned them on a chopstick while I used my laptop as the center of mass.
